Our Commitments
✦
Be Here

Show up consistently and strive to actively take part in the group. Presence is a form of love — we commit to being here for one another week after week.

✦
Be Honest

Pursue real relationships with each other and not a put-together version of yourself. We are a safe place for the real you — struggles, doubts, and all.

✦
Be Holy

Strive to practice the spiritual disciplines that stir our affections for Jesus. We fast together, study the Word together, and hold each other to a higher call.

✦
Be Hospitable

Have a sent-ness mentality and share Jesus with the world. We open our homes and our lives to show the love of Jesus to our neighbors and city.
